STEP 3: Visual Check and Head Motion Estimation
----------------------------------------------------------------

* **Input Directories for Visual Check**:
    Click the *Utilities* button, then *Visual Check*. Select the folders as input directories where `STEP 2`_ outputs.

* **Visual Check**:
    Check the output. Arrow keys can switch the images among different timepoints or subjects during Visual Check. The images will be saved automatically.
 
* **Head Motion Estimation**:
    Click the *Utilities* button, then *Head Motion Est*. Select the folders as input directories where `STEP 2`_ outputs. The results will be saved as *brant_headmotion_result.csv* and *brant_headmotion_exclusions.txt* automatically.
